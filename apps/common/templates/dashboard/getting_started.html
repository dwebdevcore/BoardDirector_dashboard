Board Director is a simple tool to help you manage {{ request.current_account.name }}'s documents, meetings, committees,

Step 1: Add Board Members.

Start by adding your first Board Members. Once you've configured the roster and you're
ready for everyone to sign in, you'll need to invite them.

Add A New Board Member

Step 2: Add First Board Meeting.

Create your first Board Meeting and upload your first agenda, minutes and reports. Once

Step 3: Upload Your First Documents.

Create folders, subfolders and upload your first documents. By default, we've added some
basic folders an organization may need. Feel free to add new, rename, or delete folders.

Upload Your First Documents

Step 4: Create Your First Committee.

Create your first committee, add a chairperson, and invite board members. By default, we've added
some committees an organization may need. Feel free to add new, rename, or delete committees.
